About

The Ben Moore Music Store was founded in 2021 with the goal of providing the central coast with access to all kinds of musical materials.

BMMS is the perfect place for all levels of musicians to find what they need to produce their art. We cater to young, old, experienced, and inexperienced musicians alike.

The users of this site love to make music and seek to fulfill their needs in a cost friendly and professional manner. Our users look foward to interacting with experienced and highly-knowledgeable employees.
